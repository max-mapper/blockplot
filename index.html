BlockPlot is a web app where you can create new 3D block worlds or upload existing worlds from other popular voxel apps and share them online with your friends.

Requirements

BlockPlot requires a web browser that supports WebGL. It will work on most computers that can play other 3D video games.

You can find out if your browser supports WebGL by visiting this test page.

Learn More

You can play BlockPlot single player without signing in, but to share your world online you will have to log in with a valid E-Mail address.
