.mca files are region files which means they contain chunks of your world. Older .mcr files (from Minecraft v1.1 and below) aren't supported by BlockPlot, but if you open them in a recent Minecraft they will be converted to .mca files.

Where can I find my region files?

Mac OS
~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves/[world-name]/region
Windows
C:\Users\[yourusernamehere]\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\saves\[world-name]\region
